Concept of Works

        过去的计划生育政策与如今再开放二胎政策导致二胎家庭中两个孩子年龄的差距较大，孩子间的交流因空间年龄性别而存在一些距离感。俩人之间的关系因这些“距离”愈发生疏。但我想说的是这些孩子之间也可以有一种属于他们的交流方式，能够缩小彼此心的距离。

        所以我为这两个孩子设计的这一款互动产品，名叫“共享之书”。这款“书”我设计成了一个盒子，一人一套。比较特殊的点是这本书不是记录本人的日常生活，而是帮自己的brother or sister 记录。

        我希望可以使他们有这样的感受：不再用时间和空间距离衡定，我们之间的距离，遥远却很近。

        The past family planning policy and the re opening of the two child policy lead to a large age gap between two children in a two child family, and there is a sense of distance between children's communication due to spatial age and gender. Because of these "distance", the relationship between the two becomes more and more sparse. But what I want to say is that these children can also have their own way of communication, which can narrow the distance between their hearts.

        So this interactive product I designed for these two kids is called shared book. I designed this "book" into a box, one set for each person. The special point is that this book is not to record my daily life, but to help my brother or sister record.

        I hope to make them feel like this: no longer using time and space distance to measure, the distance between us is far away but very close.


Introduction

现存现象situation        

        过去的计划生育政策与如今再开放二胎政策----导致孩子年龄的差距较大，孩子间的交流因空间年龄性别而存在一些距离感。且部分家庭会有大孩单方面忍让回避弟弟妹妹，幼子女则是在缺少长兄长姐陪伴的环境中与其愈发生疏。

        经过翻阅文献发现，相关案例以及调查研究基本都是针对于年龄相差不大的幼龄长子女二胎家庭的一些问题与解决办法。而对于相差太大的则一般就没有太多相关资料与解决办法。所以我希望为这些家庭找到一种沟通方式。

        The family planning policy in the past and the second child policy now open again lead to a big gap in children's age, and there is a sense of distance between children's communication due to spatial age and gender. Moreover, some families have older children who are willing to avoid their younger brothers and sisters, while younger children are more and more estranged from them in the absence of elder brother and sister's company.

        After reading the literature, it is found that the relevant cases and research are basically aimed at some problems and solutions of the two child families with small difference in age. But for the difference is too big, generally there is not too much relevant information and solutions. So I hope to find a way for these families to communicate.

Purpose and Vision

        的确因为种种原因，导致孩子之间之间不太亲密，但现实生活中存在着一些例子是找到了属于他们自己的沟通方式。并且我看到了有一些关于儿童互动玩具和二胎家庭的家具设计。

        所以我的想法是为这些因为存在空间时间距离的二胎家庭中的孩子门设计一款产品，促进孩子之间的交流，缩小他们间心的距离。

        It is true that for various reasons, children are not very close, but there are some examples in real life that they have found their own way of communication. And I saw some furniture designs for children's interactive toys and two child families.

        So my idea is to design a product for the children's door in the two child family because of the space and time distance, so as to promote the communication between children and narrow the distance between them.


Target Users

Objective

产出：

设计出供孩子之间记录和阅读的“共享书” 这本书主要是希望孩子想着对方而记录（一人一个盒子，一套产品）

实现目标：

1.让两个孩子跨越时空，年龄等距离，了解彼此的日常生活琐事

2.让孩子学会主动与对方交流，感情升温

3.父母也一同参与其中，促进家庭和谐

4.同时用触觉嗅觉等器官的感觉刺激来促使使用者更好享受生活

Output:

design a "shared book" for children to record and read. This book is mainly for children to record (a box, a set of productsfor one child) by thinking about each other

Achieve the goal:

1. Let the two children understand each other's daily life by crossing the space-time, age and other distances

2. Let the children learn to communicate with each other actively, and their feelings will warm up

3. Parents also participate in it to promote family harmony

4. At the same time, I also decide to use the sense stimulation of sense organs such as touch and smell to promote users to enjoy life better

Design and Development

*过程：

1.

        在设计书时，一开始我想到了用造句的方式，可以用一些提示语去引导使用者造句记录生活。还有一点，在完成这本书时，不一定只有文字，还有影像录音方式，以及各种可储存标本方式，还可以用颜色和几何图形去描述自己的心情，以及用刺绣针织的形式展现内心。

*Process:

1.

        When designing the book, at first, I thought of making sentences. I could use some hints to guide users to make sentences and record their lives. In addition, when finishing this book, there are not only words, but also video recording and various storage methods of specimens. You can also use color and geometric graphics to describe your mood, and use embroidery and knitting to show your heart.

*之后，因为书的形式不够特殊，所以在思考如何使他们的交流由被动化为主动时，我想到，他们在书写这本书时，完全可以是为对方记录的，也就是记录对方的生活，这样一来，这本书的内容主语就改变了，变成了我的xx。 

*而且在完成这本书时，因为是记录对方的生活，所以势必要询问对方过得如何，也就达到了使得俩人交流的目的。

*而由于使用群体还包含着幼龄孩子，所以家长有时候的陪同参与也不可缺少，也能使父母在旁听闻俩个孩子的沟通，了解自己的孩子。

2.

* At first, I only wanted to design a book, but in the end, I opened my mind through the teacher's lecture. At the same time, I thought that I could make a material package from the smell package. * Later, because the form of the book is not special enough, when thinking about how to make their communication from passive to active, I thought that when they wrote this book, they could record each other's life. In this way, the subject of the book changed and became my XX.

* And when finishing this book, because it is to record each other's life, it is necessary to ask each other how they are doing, so as to achieve the purpose of communication between them.

*As the user group also includes young children, sometimes parents' accompanying participation is indispensable, which can also make parents listen to the communication between two children and know their own children.

Test

        设计之后，我找了身边符合要求的孩子，与他一起完成项目测试。

        我先给他介绍了产品以及怎么使用，接着我们一起完成了几页内容。还模拟了打电话沟通的过程。 还有使用了颜色来表达其他事物，比如在画其中一页内容时，选择了用颜色代替meal pal de的名字等等。

        可以看见的是在制作过程中他很开心，而且他说下次还要画。

        而我在此过程中 同样更加了解了他，比如他的爱好，他喜欢的颜色，他喜欢的食物和玩具等等。

        After designing , I found the children who met the requirements and completed the project test with him.

        We first introduced him to the product and how to use it, and then we finished several pages together. The process of telephone communication is also simulated. In addition, color is used to express other things, such as choosing color instead of the name of mealpal de when drawing one of the pages.

        What you can see is that he was very happy in the production process, and he said he would like to paint next time.

        And I also got to know him better in the process, such as his hobbies, his favorite colors, his favorite food and toys, and so on.
